Tynass Trips Beta version

Tynass Trips is a mobile application that provides our users with Storyline Trips based on real historical facts through Augmented Reality, users will be provided with a list of places that they will visit and be guided by our audio narrator and they will be able to see and interact with historical characters and models in the journey.
